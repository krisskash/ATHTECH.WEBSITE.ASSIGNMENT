Food

5 foods in greece we recommend you try out

1. Moussaka

Moussaka is a layered casserole made with eggplant, minced meat (often lamb or beef), tomatoes, and b√©chamel sauce. It is seasoned with herbs and spices, creating a savory and comforting dish.




2. Souvlaki

Souvlaki consists of skewered and grilled meat, typically served with pita bread and accompanied by tomatoes, onions, and a generous drizzle of tzatziki sauce. Common meats used include pork, chicken, or lamb.




3. Dolma (Dolmades)

Dolma refers to grape leaves stuffed with a mixture of rice, pine nuts, and herbs. Sometimes, they may also contain minced meat. Dolma is often served with a side of yogurt and lemon.




4. Spanakopita

Spanakopita is a savory pastry filled with spinach, feta cheese, onions, and various herbs. The filling is encased in layers of phyllo dough, creating a flaky and flavorful pie.




5. Greek Salad (Horiatiki Salata)

A classic Greek salad is a refreshing combination of tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, olives, and feta cheese. It's seasoned with olive oil, oregano, and sometimes adorned with a few sprigs of fresh mint.
